Stuffed Moose Heart - Canadian delitrocity

Description

The Canadians heart their moose so much they stuff their hearts with love and care. Then they eat them.

Ingredients

  • Cleaned, trimmed and roasted moose heart 1
  • Bread crumbs 375g
  • Onion chopped finely 1
  • Italian herbs or any other seasoning of your choice 2 Tbsp
  • Melted butter 1/4 cup

Steps:

  1. Mix the crumbs, chopped onion, seasoning, butter together and stuff in the heart.
  2. Bake at 350 degrees for 1.5 hours, or until heart is tender.
  3. TIP: Wrap heart in parchment paper and foil to prevent drying out.
