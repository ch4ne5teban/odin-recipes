Criadillas - Fried testicles - Spanish delitrocity

Description

Have some balls? I mean fried ones of a bull, calf or a pig? "Criadillas" are fried testicles - a delitrocity commonplace in bull-fighting countries like Spain, Argentina, and Mexico.

Ingredients

  • Bulls testicles 500g
  • Garlic cloves 5
  • Egg 1
  • Bread crumbs 60g
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Thyme
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Filet the criadillas using a sharp knife. Remove the skin and cut each testicle into 4, length-ways.
  2. Bring a salted large pot of water to the boil and place the criadillas into the pot. Cover and leave to simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Whilst your criadillas are cooking, in a separate bowl combine the breadcrumbs with garlic, thyme, cayenne pepper, salt and black pepper and mix well.
  4. Remove the criadillas from the pan and pat dry, leaving for around 5 minutes.
  5. Fill a frying pan with enough oil to shallow fry and pre-heat.
  6. Crack an egg in to a separate bowl and whisk briefly. Now dip each of your criadillas into the egg and then into the breadcrumb mix.
  7. Once they are all coated, pop them into the frying pan for around 3 minutes or until they float to the surface.
  8. Take them out of the pan and serve with salsa.
